Pre-Project Teacher Resources

Student Self-Learning

Prior to the project, students work on learning HTML & CSS through online platforms. We chose to use CodeCademy. Students complete tutorials on a weekly basis for homework, and review material prior to weekly concept checks. Below are the tutorials students go through on a weekly basis. Note: If internet connectivity is an issue, weekly assignments are short enough to complete in class one day a week.
